Farmer Help Center

Everything you need to know about selling on Bhoomi Mitra.

🌾 How to sell your crops?

Click the "Post Crop" button, enter your crop name, quantity, price, and location. Contractors will then see your listing and send you offers.

🤝 How to manage offers?

When a contractor shows interest, it will appear under "Interested Contractors". You can Accept the deal or Negotiate a better price.

💬 How to chat?

Click the "Messages" link in the top bar to see all your active conversations with contractors.

🤖 What can the Chatbot do?

The chatbot provides weather updates, latest MSP prices, crop-specific sowing advice, and info on government schemes.
