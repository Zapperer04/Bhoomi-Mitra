🌱 Farmer Help Center

Everything you need to know about managing your crops and deals.

📢 Posting a Crop

Click on "Post Crop" in your dashboard. Fill in the details like crop type, variety, and quantity. Setting a fair market price helps attract more buyers quickly. Your listing will be visible to all verified contractors immediately.

🤝 Managing Interests

When a buyer is interested, you'll see them in your "Interested Contractors" list. You can view their profile and offered price. You have the power to Accept, Reject, or Negotiate the price through our secure chat.

💬 Secure Negotiation

Use the built-in chat to discuss logistics, quality, and delivery dates. Never share your personal bank details or phone number outside the platform for your own safety. Our system logs all agreements to protect you.

✅ Finalizing Deals

Once both parties agree, the deal is locked. After you deliver the goods and receive payment, mark the deal as Completed. This helps you build a high trust rating, making it easier to sell in the future.

🤖 AI Assistant

Need to know the weather or MSP rates? Use our Bhoomi Mitra Chatbot. It speaks multiple Indian languages and can guide you on the best time to sell or what fertilizers to use.

🏛️ Government Schemes

Check the Gov Schemes section regularly. We fetch the latest agricultural benefits and subsidies directly from official sources to ensure you don't miss out on financial aid.
