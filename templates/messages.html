Farmer Help Center

🤝 How to manage offers?

You can Accept the deal or Counter Offer if you want a different price or quantity. Use the chat to discuss terms.

✅ Closing the Deal

Once both parties accept, the deal is closed. You can then mark "Payment Received" when the contractor pays you.

Contractor Help Center

📉 How to negotiate?

Use Counter Offer to propose new terms. If the farmer counters, you'll see a notification in the chat bar.

✅ Finalizing

After the deal is accepted by both, use the chat to coordinate delivery. You can mark "Goods Received" once you get the crop.
