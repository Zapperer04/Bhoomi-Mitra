Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set the right price for my crop?

We recommend checking the current MSP (Minimum Support Price) using our Chatbot. Additionally, look at other similar listings in your region to ensure your price is competitive but fair.

What happens if a buyer doesn't pay?

Never deliver goods before payment is confirmed or an escrow agreement is reached. If a buyer defaults, use the "File a Complaint" box on this page immediately. We track all user behavior and will ban fraudulent buyers.

Can I edit my crop listing after posting?

Yes, you can edit the price and quantity from your dashboard at any time. Note that changing details might affect active negotiations.

How do I increase my Farmer Rating?

Ratings improve when you complete deals successfully. Providing accurate crop descriptions, being responsive in chat, and fulfilling orders on time are the best ways to get 5-star reviews.
