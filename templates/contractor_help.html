🤝 Contractor Help Center

Your guide to sourcing quality crops and managing procurement deals.

🔍 Sourcing Crops

Use the marketplace search and filters to find the exact crop and variety you need. You can see real-time listings from verified farmers across the region, including quantity and expected price.

🙋 Showing Interest

When you find a suitable crop, click "Show Interest". You'll need to specify your procurement quantity and offer price. The farmer will receive an instant notification to review your proposal.

📉 Negotiation Strategy

Farmers may send counter-offers if they want to adjust the price or quantity. You can continue the negotiation in our secure chat. Keep an eye on your Messages for status updates.

🛡️ Trust & Verification

Bhoomi Mitra verifies all farmers on the platform. However, we recommend checking a farmer's history and ratings before finalizing large deals. This ensures a reliable supply chain for your procurement.

📦 Finalizing Procurement

Once a deal is accepted, coordinate the logistics through the chat. After you receive the goods and verify quality, mark the deal as Completed to finalize the transaction records.

📊 Market Insights

Use the Chatbot to get current market trends and MSP prices. This information can help you make more informed bidding decisions and manage your budget effectively.
