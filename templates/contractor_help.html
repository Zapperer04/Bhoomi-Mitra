Frequently Asked Questions

How can I verify crop quality before buying?

Use the secure chat to request real-time photos or videos of the harvest. You can also arrange for a sample inspection. Once delivered, verify the goods before confirming receipt on the platform.

What if the farmer changes the price after acceptance?

Prices are legally binding once both parties accept the deal. If a farmer attempts to change terms outside the platform, do not proceed and report the listing using the complaint box.

Can I cancel an offer I've sent?

Yes, you can withdraw an interest as long as the farmer hasn't accepted it yet. Go to "My Interests" and click "Withdraw".
